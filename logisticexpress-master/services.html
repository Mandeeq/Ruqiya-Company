Our Services

What We Can Do For You

CUSTOMS CLEARANCE

We handle customs processes for imports, exports, and transit at Kenya’s ports and borders. Our team works with key authorities to ensure compliance and fast clearance of your goods.

INTERNATIONAL FREIGHT FORWARDING

We deliver complete logistics from origin to destination through a trusted global network—ensuring service that's safe, timely and completely hassle-free.

SPECIAL CARGO SERVICES

With deep industry experience, we manage fragile, oversized, and hazardous cargo using the right equipment and expertise. We ensure safe, compliant, and cost-effective delivery tailored to each shipment’s unique needs.

AIRFREIGHT SERVICES

We provide fast, reliable airfreight clearance for urgent imports and exports. Our expertise and airline partnerships ensure swift documentation and delivery within days.

LOGISTIC SUPPLY CHAIN SOLUTIONS

We deliver tailored, efficient supply chain solutions to cut costs, boost performance, and ensure smooth logistics—from inventory to distribution.

PACKING AND REMOVAL SERVICES

Our skilled team ensures safe, secure packing and crating for all cargo—fragile or large. Ideal for relocations and diplomats, we guarantee your items arrive intact and protected.
